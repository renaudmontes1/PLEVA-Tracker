About PLEVA Tracker

What is PLEVA Tracker?

PLEVA Tracker is a specialized iOS application designed to help patients monitor and manage PLEVA (Pityriasis Lichenoides et Varioliformis Acuta) symptoms. Whether you're newly diagnosed or managing ongoing symptoms, our app provides the tools you need for comprehensive symptom tracking and analysis.
