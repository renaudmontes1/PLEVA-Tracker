Privacy Policy for PLEVA Tracker

Last Updated: September 24, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to PLEVA Tracker. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This privacy policy describes how we handle your information when you use our iOS application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Health and Medical Information

We collect and store:

  • PLEVA symptom severity ratings
  • Papule count data for different body regions
  • Notes about affected areas
  • Symptom progression data

2.2 Photos

With your permission, we access:

  • Photos you take or select for documenting symptoms
  • Camera access for capturing new symptom images

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for:

  • Tracking and monitoring your PLEVA symptoms over time
  • Generating trend analysis and progress reports
  • Creating AI-powered weekly summaries using Azure OpenAI
  • Providing visual representations of your condition's progression

4. Data Storage and Security

We prioritize the security of your personal health information:

  • All health data is stored locally on your device
  • Photos are stored securely in your device's photo library
  • Data transmitted to Azure OpenAI for analysis is encrypted in transit
  • We do not store your personal health information on external servers

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Azure OpenAI: Used for generating weekly symptom summaries
    • Only anonymized symptom data is processed
    • No personally identifiable information is shared
    • Data processing complies with Azure's security standards

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access your health data stored in the app
  • Delete your data from the app
  • Control photo and camera access permissions

7. Data Retention

Your data remains on your device until you choose to delete it. You can delete your data at any time through the app's settings.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
